CHINESE HERBAL MEDICINE
CHINESE HERBAL MEDICINE

Herbology is one of the most important modalities used in Traditional Chinese Medicine. Each herbal prescription is a cocktail of many herbs tailored to the individual patient. The patient's internal balance is taken into account when the herbs are selected. The herbs incorporate ingredients from all parts of plants: leaf, stem, bark, flower, fruit, seed and root.

PHYSICAL THERAPY SERVICES

By partnering with Healers of Motion Physical Therapy, we now offer services for sports injuries, surgical recovery rehabilitation, lymphedema treatment, and chronic conditions as lower back pain, sciatic pain, neck pain, TMJ disorder causing headaches, carpal tunnel syndrome, tennis elbow, knee pain, shoulders disorders (torn rotator cuff, frozen shoulder, etc), and any other joint problems.

JOIN PAIN THERAPY
JOIN PAIN THERAPY

Physical Therapy improves the mobility and restores the use of affected joints, increasing strength to support the joints, and maintain fitness and the ability to perform daily activities.

Lymphedema Treatment
Lymphedema Treatment

prevent and reduce the swelling associated with lymphedema. Treatment may include skin care, manual lymph drainage, gentle massage, and light exercises to help stimulate the lymphatic system.

Oncology Rehab
Oncology Rehab

Designing an individualized exercise program for pre-post oncology patients combining range of motion training with light resistance exercises, which reduce fatigue and improve physical function, safety and well-being.

TMD Therapy
TMD Therapy

The focus of physical therapy for temporomandibular disorders (TMDs) is relaxation, stretching, releasing tight muscles and scar tissue in order to improve maximal functionality in the temporomandibular joint.
