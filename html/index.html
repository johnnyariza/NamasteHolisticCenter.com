ACUPUNCTURE BALANCE METHOD
ACUPUNCTURE BALANCE METHOD

Balance Method Acupuncture unique is that it provides the following: A sense of Balance in your life, 90% to 95% success rate through a series of treatments, No need of needle insertion on the affected area; which is already painful and swelling, Faster overall recovery times, Pain relief within seconds of needle insertion.

AURICULAR ACUPUNCTURE
AURICULAR ACUPUNCTURE

One of the modalities of Traditional Acupuncture specially used for Smoking Cessation and Weight Loss Treatments.

ACUPUNCTURE FOR PREGNANCY
ACUPUNCTURE IN PREGNANCY

In our clinic, we give you personalized acupuncture treatments for infertility and help you conceive. In pregnancy, acupuncture can treat morning sickness, constipation, back pain, fetal malposition (breech and shoulder presentations), and overdue pregnancy.

MOXIBUSTION
MOXIBUSTION

Moxibustion is also an ancient modality used for both prevention and healing of disease. It works through burning moxa or mugwort, which is an herb, to warm the Meridians and Acupuncture Points. It can help improve the flow of Energy (Chi) and blood, achieving superior healing effects; relieve pain, relax the muscles, boost energy.

CUPPING
CUPPING

Cupping is a very effective healing treatment to relax tightness and tension of muscles by dispelling stagnant blood and Chi (energy). Cupping also releases deep toxins and improves the circulation of energy to treat respiratory conditions, such as common cold, pneumonia, bronchitis and uncomplicated cases of asthma.

ELECTROACUPUNCTURE
ELECTROACUPUNCTURE

Electroacupuncture is a tecnique where a small electric current is passed between pairs of needles. This augments the effects of acupuncture in order to restore health and well-being. Electroacupuncture is considered to be especially useful for conditions in which there is an accumulation of chi(Energy), such as in chronic pain conditions(tendinitis, bursitis), severe muscle spasm (back pain).

HOMEOPATHIC INJECTIONS
HOMEOPATHIC INJECTION THERAPY

This technique involves the injection of homeopathic remedies into specific areas of the body. The remedies are designed to stimulate natural healing rather than suppressing symptoms. This evokes a healing response in the body which allows for the resolution of the symptoms.

CHINESE HERBAL MEDICINE
CHINESE HERBAL MEDICINE

Herbology is one of the most important modalities used in Traditional Chinese Medicine. Each herbal prescription is a cocktail of many herbs tailored to the individual patient. The patient's internal balance is taken into account when the herbs are selected. The herbs incorporate ingredients from all parts of plants: leaf, stem, bark, flower, fruit, seed and root.
