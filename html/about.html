"The Spirit Within Me, Greets and Honors, The Spirit Within You"

Founder of Namaste Pembroke Pines Acupuncture & Physical Therapy, Dr. Sandra Mora is a Licensed Acupuncture Physician in the state of Florida and a Diplomate in Oriental Medicine, as designated by the NCCAOM (National Certification Commission for Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine). She received the designation for diplomat status in both Acupuncture and Chinese Herbology. She holds certification in Acupuncture Injection Therapy and Acupuncture Facial Rejuvenation.

Sandra got her training in the oldest acupuncture school in Florida (Acupuncture and Massage College of Miami). During her course, she had the opportunity to intern under the direction of Chinese Professors/Doctors who came to this country from Beijing and Shanghai Medical Universities of China. During her training, she was one of the three national USA recipients for the scholarships awarded by the Asian Medicine Institute (California), for one of the best GPA, student performance, and essay about "Leadership and Traditional Chinese Medicine".

Dr. Mora has a background in Western Medicine as well as having practiced for 10 years as a Medical Doctor in Colombia (South America), her country of origin. In U.S.A., she is a retired Cardiac Sonographer from the ARDMS (American Registry for Diagnostic Medical Sonography).

Dr. Mora graduated with honors, received the Valedictorian Award of Excellence, and earned a Master's Degree in Oriental Medicine. She is a former Acupuncture Clinic Supervisor of the AMC (Acupuncture and Massage College of Miami). Currently, she works at her clinic NAMASTE PEMBROKE PINES ACUPUNCTURE & PHYSICAL THERAPY in Pembroke Pines - Florida.

Dr. Sandra Mora has dedicated herself to this field, practicing with Love, Compassion, and Service.
